# Jonathan Barbeau — Generative Artist

This site is the official portfolio and presence of **Jonathan Barbeau**, a generative artist and creative developer based in Québec City, Canada.

## Who This Is

Jonathan creates generative art using JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas. His work includes long-form collections on fx(hash), Foundation, and Highlight, as well as exhibitions and installations (e.g. Je me souviens, STARMAP, Traces, EX-LIBRIS, Jeu de Ligne, Exhibit-A). He also works as a creative front-end developer.

## What This Site Is

- **Portfolio**: Recent projects, descriptions, and links to view or acquire work.
- **Blog**: Articles and updates (hosted on Substack, syndicated here).
- **CV**: Artist curriculum (exhibitions, residencies, publications, list of works) in English and French; technical CV also available.
